I am a final year graduate student pursuing MS in Computer Science from University of Maryland, Baltimore County. My current research interests lie in Quantum Computing. My current reserach work under Dr. Ajinkya Borle is in the implementation of the Biclustering algorithm using photonic quantum computing. Prior to this work I was also engaged in various other academic projects and research. I have published papers on quantum annealing and the integration of brain-computer interfaces with virtual reality. My recent projects include simulations of the HHL algorithm using Qiskit, the development of a quantum-accelerated simulated annealing algorithm, and a literature review on quantum natural language processing.

Before joining UMBC I completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from University of Mumbai, in 2018. Following my undergraduate studies, I worked as an Application Development Analyst at Accenture in Mumbai from October 2018 to November 2021.
